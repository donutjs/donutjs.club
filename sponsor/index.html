Sponsoring DonutJS!

Each month DonutJS is sponsored by 2-3 generous companies! We use these funds to cover food, venue costs, and equipment. All leftover proceeds from ticket sales and outside funds are donated to a non-profit in the community. Sponsorship starts at $500 for one month, with discounts for companies that would like to sponsor for multiple months.

Sponsorship includes:

  • 2 minutes to speak to the audience about your product, hiring, etc.
  • A space at the event to give out swag
  • Your logo on our website and in our emails to attendees

If you’d like to be a sponsor, please contact us at hello@donutjs.club
